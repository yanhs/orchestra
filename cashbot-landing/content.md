## 10. FAQ

### Section headline
**Questions.**

---

**Q1: Is CashBot only for designers? What about developers and writers?**

CashBot was built for any service-based freelancer. The Smart Pricing engine has specialized models for UX/UI design, web and mobile development, copywriting, content strategy, consulting, and video production. The proposal templates adapt to your niche — a development proposal looks and reads differently from a copywriting one. If you sell a service to a client, CashBot fits your workflow.

---

**Q2: How accurate is the Smart Pricing feature? Where does the data come from?**

Smart Pricing pulls from a continuously updated dataset of 200,000+ real freelance projects, broken down by service type, geography, experience level, and project scope. It's not a generic salary survey — it's project-level data from actual freelance markets. You'll get a low/mid/high range with a recommendation, not a single number. You always make the final call. Most users tell us the estimates track very closely to what they were already charging — and often show them where they were underpriced.

---

**Q3: Can my clients sign proposals online? Is it legally binding?**

Yes, on the Pro plan. CashBot generates proposals with an embedded e-signature field. When your client signs, both parties receive a timestamped PDF confirmation to their email. The signature process uses audit trail logging (IP address, timestamp, device) that meets electronic signature standards in the US, EU (eIDAS), UK, Canada, and Australia. For enterprise contracts requiring advanced e-signatures, we recommend following up with a dedicated legal tool — but for standard freelance agreements, CashBot's signing is sufficient.

---

**Q4: What payment methods can my clients use?**

On Pro, clients can pay via Stripe (credit/debit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay) or PayPal directly from the invoice link. You connect your Stripe or PayPal account in Settings — money goes straight to you, CashBot never holds funds. We charge no transaction fees on top of what Stripe or PayPal charges. You keep what you invoice.

---

**Q5: I'm already using FreshBooks / Bonsai / HoneyBook. Why switch?**

Those are solid tools. The difference is AI. CashBot doesn't just generate a blank invoice template — it writes your proposal, prices your project based on market data, and creates the invoice from the approved scope without any manual re-entry. If you're happy spending an hour on proposals and pricing manually, your current tool is fine. If that time is the cost of staying solo, CashBot is the alternative.
